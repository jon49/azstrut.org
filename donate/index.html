Drop-Off Locations and Hours


Companies and the public can drop-off computers, hard drives, monitors, printers and other computer equipment at the StRUT collection centers. We accept all computer electronics, also personal electronics such as stereo equipment, DVD, VCR players, etc. Please see our Hard Drive Security Statement for more information on our computer security processses. Drop-Offs are also accepted at Batteries Plus nine valley store locations.
